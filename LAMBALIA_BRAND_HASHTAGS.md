# üí° PRO TIPS

## Best Practices:

1. **Consistency is Key**
   - Always use #Lambalia as first hashtag
   - Keep 2-3 brand tags in every post

2. **Mix Sizes**
   - Large hashtags (1M+ posts): 20%
   - Medium hashtags (100K-1M): 50%
   - Small hashtags (10K-100K): 30%

3. **First Comment Strategy**
   - Put hashtags in first comment (Instagram)
   - Keeps caption clean
   - Same algorithm benefits

4. **Create Branded CTAs**
   - "Tag us with #Lambalia"
   - "Share your #LambaliaSuccess story"
   - "Join the #LambaliaFamily"

5. **Local Variations**
   - #LambaliaUSA, #LambaliaLagos, #LambaliaMexico
   - For geo-targeted campaigns
