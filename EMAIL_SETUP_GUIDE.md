## 🛠️ How to Set Up Email Accounts

### Option 1: Through Your Domain Registrar/Hosting Provider

1. **Log into your hosting control panel** (cPanel, Plesk, or similar)
2. **Find "Email Accounts" or "Email"** section
3. **Create New Email Account** for each address above
4. **Set the generic passwords** as listed above
5. **Configure mailbox size** (recommend at least 5GB each)

### Option 2: Google Workspace (Recommended for Business)

1. **Visit**: https://workspace.google.com/
2. **Sign up** for Google Workspace using your lambalia.net domain
3. **Add users** for each email address
4. **Cost**: ~$6/month per user (5 users = $30/month)
5. **Benefits**: 
   - Professional Gmail interface
   - 30GB storage per account
   - Integration with Google Calendar, Drive, Meet
   - Mobile app support
   - Advanced spam protection

### Option 3: Microsoft 365 Business

1. **Visit**: https://www.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/business
2. **Choose Business Basic** ($6/month per user)
3. **Add your domain** and verify ownership
4. **Create accounts** for each email address
5. **Benefits**:
   - Outlook interface
   - 50GB mailbox per user
   - Teams integration
   - Office apps access

## 🔐 Security Recommendations

### Strong Password Policy
- **Change generic passwords** immediately after setup
- **Use unique passwords** for each account
- **Enable 2FA** if supported by your email provider
- **Consider password manager** like 1Password or Bitwarden

### Email Security Features
- **Enable spam filtering**
- **Set up DKIM and SPF records** for your domain
- **Enable DMARC** to prevent email spoofing
- **Regular security audits**

## 📱 Mobile Setup

### For iPhone/iPad:
1. **Settings** → **Mail** → **Accounts** → **Add Account**
2. **Choose "Other"** → **Add Mail Account**
3. **Enter email and password**
4. **Configure IMAP/SMTP settings** from your provider

### For Android:
1. **Gmail app** → **Settings** → **Add account**
2. **Choose "Other"** 
3. **Enter email credentials**
4. **Configure server settings**
